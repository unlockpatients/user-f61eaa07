Privacy & Security Policy

Last Updated: September 25, 2025

WonderKidz Pediatrics ("us", "we", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our patients and website visitors. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect information in the following ways:

  • Personal Information: When you contact us by phone or through our website, we may collect personal details such as your name, your child's name, phone number, and email address to schedule appointments and respond to your inquiries.
  • Non-Personal Information (Analytics): We use third-party analytics services, such as Google Ads and PostHog, to understand how visitors use our site. This may include information about your browser, device type, and browsing patterns. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. We have configured these services to not collect IP addresses.
  • Cookies: Our website and the third-party services we use may use cookies to help analyze web traffic and improve our services. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide patient care and schedule appointments.
  • To communicate with you about your inquiries or appointments.
  • To improve our website's functionality and user experience.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our online advertising.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Security and HIPAA

We take the security of your information very seriously. As a healthcare provider, we are compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal and protected health information (PHI).

  • Our website is secured with SSL/HTTPS encryption to protect data transmitted to and from the site.
  • We have implemented technical safeguards, including a Content Security Policy (CSP), to prevent unauthorized scripts and resources from running on our website, protecting you from cross-site scripting (XSS) and other attacks.
  • Access to any personal or health information is restricted to authorized staff who need the information to perform a specific job.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for advertising and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies, and we have chosen partners who are also committed to data protection. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy & Security Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
