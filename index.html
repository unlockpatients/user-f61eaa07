Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pediatric services

What services do you offer for newborns?

+

We provide comprehensive newborn care including well-baby check-ups, vaccinations, feeding guidance, and development monitoring. Our pediatricians are experienced in caring for the youngest patients and helping new parents through the early stages.

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office. We offer both in-person and telehealth appointments.

What should I bring to my child's first appointment?

+

Please bring your child's insurance card, immunization records, medical history, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records from previous providers. Arriving 15 minutes early helps us complete necessary paperwork.

Do you accept insurance?

+

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Medicaid. Please contact our office to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan.

What ages do you treat?

+

We care for children from birth through adolescence (0-18 years). Our pediatricians are trained to address the unique health needs of each developmental stage and provide age-appropriate care.

Do you offer same-day sick appointments?

+

Yes, we reserve slots each day for same-day sick visits. Please call our office early in the day to schedule a same-day appointment for your child if they are ill.
